Basic Crepes

Description

This is a simple and quick to make crepe batter from ingredients you already have!

Preparation time will take 10 minutes, cook time needs 20; resulting to a warm belly in 30. This recipe yields 8 crepes and serves 4.

Per Serving: 216 calories; protein 7.4g; carbohydrates 25.5g; fat 9.2g; cholesterol 110.7mg; sodium 235.3mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and the eggs. Gradually add in the milk and water, stirring to combine. Add the salt and butter; beat until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  3. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side. Serve hot.
